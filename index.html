SUPER TROFEO WORLD FINAL

The Lamborghini World Final has brought the curtain down on every Super Trofeo season since 2013. Every year, the drivers from all of the continental championships go to a different circuit and take part in two 50-minute Sprint races, with the overall winner being crowned as the Lamborghini world champion. As well as being the biggest racing weekend of the year, the Lamborghini World Final is a genuine motorsport festival that attracts fans and owners from all over the world.

The owner's parade, previews and paddock entertainment for fans are just some of the activities organized for the event.

SUPER TROFEO TEAMS

All of the cars that compete in the Lamborghini Super Trofeo championships are driven by private teams that use their expertise to fine-tune them for the drivers. For each Lamborghini Super Trofeo continental championship, there are driver and team rankings.

Drivers and teams participating in the Continental Series compete each year in the Lamborghini Grand Final, the event that concludes the Super Trofeo sporting season. Two qualifying sessions and two races determine the world champions of the series. Each year, the Grand Final is held at a different track, and in past editions, it has touched the European, Asian and American continents.

Super Trofeo Europe 2022 season schedule

The 14th in the history of the GT series, dedicated to the Lamborghini Huracan Super Trofeo Evo cars. Six stages, a total of 12 races and 600 minutes of competition will be played out on some of the most prestigious tracks in Europe.

Stage 1: Imola, Italy April 1-3
Stage 2: Le Castellet, France June 3-5
Stage 3: Misano, Italy July 1-3
Stage 4: Spa-Francorchamps, Belgium July 29-30
Stage 5: Barcelona, Spain September 30-October 2
Stage 6: Portimao, Portugal November 3-4
Grand Final 2022: Portimao, Portugal November 5-6

PORTIMAO TRACK

The Autodromo Internacional do Algarve is located in the region of the same name in the south of Portugal, near the city of Portimão. Opened in October 2008, the racing complex is one of the most modern in Europe, spread over an area of about 300 hectares among the hills surrounding the Portuguese city, and the length of the track itself is 4,684 meters.

SUPER TROFEO EVO

The new Huracán Super Trofeo EVO achieves even greater heights than its illustrious predecessor thanks to completely redesigned aerodynamics. The Huracán Super Trofeo EVO is the ideal launching pad for all drivers who want to begin their GT-class racing careers.

The Huracán EVO is the evolution of the most successful Lamborghini with a V10 engine. As a result of the fine-tuning and refinement of existing features, combined with new design solutions that enhance performance, the car stands out for its ability to anticipate and satisfy driver behavior, expectations and desires.

Its inimitable design is a natural evolution of the iconic Huracán lines, incorporating sophisticated new solutions that elevate performance and evoke emotion to a level never seen before.

The unprecedented dodger was developed by the engineers of Automobili Lamborghini's motorsport department in collaboration with Dallara Engineering and Lamborghini Centro Stile. The latter also designed the starting livery for the car in honor of the partnership between Lamborghini Squadra Corse and Roger Dubuis, which began in 2018.

Among the most obvious changes to the Lamborghini Huracan Super Trofeo EVO dodger are the rear spoiler on the rear hood and the upper air intake, which make it instantly recognizable to onlookers.
